Parking - Limited parking is available at the chapel and reception sites. However, some may prefer to leave cars in one of the many parking decks downtown as these will be closer to the reception. The parking decks are free on the weekends and are still within a 5 to 10 minute walk of the chapel. Street parking is also free on weekends, and is typically available on sunday evenings. Parking on the street near the venues is also a good option! The nearby parking decks are shown on the map for both locations.
